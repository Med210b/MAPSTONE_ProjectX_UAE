Here instructions on how to use this folder:

1. Upload your project picture files (like .jpg, .png) into this exact folder (`public/images/projects/`).
2. Inside your `src/data/projects.ts` file, you can link the picture to your project by using this path format:
   
   imageUrl: '/images/projects/your-file-name.jpg'

That's it! The app will automatically read the picture from here.
